Drums – AI Music Improviser

Tenori-Off is an AI-powered music sequencer that generates new musical patterns based on user input. Users create a melody on a grid and the AI model improvises variations in real time.

How it Works

The system uses neural networks from Magenta.js to analyze rhythm and melody patterns. Once the user enters notes into the grid, the AI predicts new notes that match the musical style.

Features

• Interactive music grid
• AI melody generation
• Real-time improvisation
• Drum and synth layers
