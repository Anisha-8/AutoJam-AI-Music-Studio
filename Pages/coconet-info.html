Coconet – AI Harmonization

Coconet is an AI model designed to generate harmonies for musical compositions. It can take an incomplete musical score and intelligently fill in missing notes to create a complete harmony.

How it Works

The system is based on a convolutional neural network trained on Bach chorales. It learns patterns of harmony and predicts missing notes in polyphonic music.

Features

• AI harmony generation
• Polyphonic music support
• Interactive music editing
• Classical style composition
